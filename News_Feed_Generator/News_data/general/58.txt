Here's what experts say is needed for US to return to normal. But these barriers stand in the way
There's no denying the rising optimism felt by some as state governors expand Covid-19 vaccination access and push to end capacity restrictions on businesses and large gatherings. However, experts are sounding the alarm when it comes to two barriers keeping the US from a return to normalcy: Covid-19 variants and vaccine hesitancy.
