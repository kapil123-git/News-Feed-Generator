Cozy Grove - Release Date Reveal Trailer - IGN
Watch the trailer for a look at this upcoming slice-of-life game, Cozy Grove, about camping on a haunted island.

In Cozy Grove, players take on the role of a Spirit Scout and must explore the island to find hidden secrets, and help both the spectral inhabitants and living residents of this small paradise with their quests. 

Cozy Grove launches on April 8, 2021, for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and S, and PC. The game is also available on Apple Arcade.
