Fall Guys’ new hoverboard stage looks like a neon nightmare
Fall Guys season 4 is on the way, due to start on March 22. Developer Mediatonic recently showed off one of the new stages that will arrive in the game. Hoverboard Heroes is a frantic race where players have to stay on their hoverboard while dodging obstacles in their way.
