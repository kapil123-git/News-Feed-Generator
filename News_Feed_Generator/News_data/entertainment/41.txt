The World Video Game Hall of Fame nominates Animal Crossing, Portal
The Strong National Museum of Play maintains the World Video Game Hall of Fame. This year’s nominees include StarCraft, Guitar Hero, Animal Crossing, and Microsoft Flight Simulator for their lasting impact on culture and entertainment.
