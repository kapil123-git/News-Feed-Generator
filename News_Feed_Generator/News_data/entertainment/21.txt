Townscaper - Announcement Trailer - IGN
Watch this calming trailer for this upcoming sandbox city builder, and get ready to create quaint island towns, hamlets, canal networks, sky cities on stilts, and more when Townscaper launches in summer 2021 for PC, Nintendo Switch, and iOS and Android devices.

The game is currently available in Steam Early Access for PC and Mac.

In Townscaper, players will select a color, plop some blocks onto the grid and watch as the game's underlying algorithm automatically turns them into cute little houses, arches, stairways, bridges, gardens, and more.
