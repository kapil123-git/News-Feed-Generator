Sony buys Evo fighting games tournament, schedules Evo Online 2021
Sony and Endeavor talent agency announced on March 18, 2021 that a new partnership they formed had acquired the Evolution Championship Series, the premier fighting games tournament begun in 2002 by co-founders Tony and Tom Cannon. The Cannons will remain involved with the tournament “as key advisors,” a statement said.
