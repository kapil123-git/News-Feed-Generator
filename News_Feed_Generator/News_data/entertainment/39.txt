Pathfinder announces a new set of seven Runelords miniatures by WizKids
The Pathfinder role-playing game is introducing a new set of miniatures. It will include all seven of the legendary Runelords, which have plagued the world of Golarian since the first edition of the game launched in 2007. Made by WizKids, they’ll go on sale this summer.
