Everything announced at the Square Enix Presents March 2021
The next game in the Life is Strange series highlighted the list of announcements during the Square Enix Presents livestream event on March 18, 2021. Other major announcements included Forspoken (originally called Project Athia), coming in 2022 for PlayStation 5 and PC, and the “War for Wakanda” expansion bringing Black Panther to Marvel’s Avengers later this year.
